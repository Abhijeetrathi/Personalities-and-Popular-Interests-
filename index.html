Discover Notable Personalities and Popular Interests

Football

Football is a globally popular team sport played with a spherical ball between two teams of eleven players.

Hockey

Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on ice or field, where players use sticks to hit a puck or ball into the opponent's goal.

Manu Bhaker

Manu Bhaker is an accomplished Indian sport shooter who has represented India in various international competitions and Olympic Games.

Karan Aujla

Karan Aujla is a popular Indian singer, songwriter, and rapper known for his work in the Punjabi music industry.

BGMI

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) is a battle royale game developed specifically for the Indian gaming community.

Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi is the current Prime Minister of India, known for his economic reforms and strong leadership on the global stage.

Apple

Apple Inc. is a leading technology company known for its innovative products like the iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch, as well as services like the App Store and iCloud.

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower.

Sidharth Malhotra

Sidharth Malhotra is an Indian actor and former model who made his Bollywood debut with the film Student of the Year in 2012.

Kiara Advani

Kiara Advani is an Indian actress known for her work in Bollywood films. She gained recognition for her roles in movies like Kabir Singh, and Shershaah.

Triptii Dimri

Triptii Dimri is an Indian actress known for her performances in Hindi cinema. She gained recognition for her roles in films like Bulbbul and Qala.

Ranbir Kapoor

Ranbir Kapoor is a popular Indian actor known for his versatile performances in Bollywood. He has won numerous awards for his roles in films like Rockstar, Barfi, and Sanju.
